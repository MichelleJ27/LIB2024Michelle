York Street Public School Library

The York Street Public School library plays an important role in the academic and social lives of the students.

MISSION

  1. To build a learning community that provides students with equitable opportunities.
  2. To reach students potential and develop into respectful, creative and knowledgeable community members who contribute to society.

    3. VISION

    1. Envisions school libraries as welcoming places where students can engage, belong, grow, learn, explore and discover.
    2. Believes in being "Better Together in a Safe and Caring Learning Community".
